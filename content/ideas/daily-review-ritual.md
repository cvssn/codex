## the questions

1. what did i actually do today?
2. what surprised me?
3. what is the one thing tomorrow needs?

## why these three

- the first one fights the feeling of having "done nothing"
- the second one captures the small unexpected moments — almost every interesting idea i've had came from answering this honestly
- the third one shrinks tomorrow to a single anchor; the rest of the list is noise around it

## form factor

a single text file per week. four lines per day, plain prose. resist tags, resist dashboards. the goal is to **think for ten minutes**, not to feed a system.

## what to skip

mood scores, weather logs, sleep numbers — they belong in their own apps, not here. this is a place for sentences only.
