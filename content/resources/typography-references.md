## books worth owning

- robert bringhurst — _the elements of typographic style_
- ellen lupton — _thinking with type_
- erik spiekermann — _stop stealing sheep & find out how type works_

## sites that show good craft

- [practical typography](https://practicaltypography.com)
- [fonts in use](https://fontsinuse.com)
- [typewolf](https://typewolf.com)

## fonts i return to

- iA writer quattro — humanist mono with proportional warmth
- jetbrains mono — clean, ligature-rich, generous x-height
- inter — for ui where neutral wins
- ibm plex serif — when warmth is needed in long-form

## a personal rule

if a page has more than two type families, there's usually a third one trying to sneak in. cut it.
